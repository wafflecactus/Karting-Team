Inside The Kids Academy

Our Kids Academy (ages 6–15) is a safe, fun and structured environment where young drivers fall in love with racing while building real skills. Sessions are grouped by age and ability so every child progresses at their own pace.

  • Small groups with a high coach-to-driver ratio
  • Full safety gear and well-maintained karts provided
  • Progressive skill levels — from first laps to club racing
  • Fundamentals: car control, racing line and track awareness
  • Confidence, discipline and sportsmanship from day one
  • A clear pathway into the EZ Racing Karting Team
Book A Taster Session

Inside The Adult Academy

The Adult Academy (16+) is built for everyone from complete beginners to club racers looking to sharpen up. Flexible sessions fit around your schedule, with personalised coaching and real data feedback.

  • Beginner, intermediate and advanced tracks
  • One-to-one and small-group coaching options
  • Race craft: starts, overtaking, defending and strategy
  • Telemetry and video review to find lap time
  • Practice days and arrive-and-drive options
  • A clear route into competitive karting and beyond
