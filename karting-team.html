How To Join The EZ Racing Team

We welcome committed drivers of all ages who want to compete seriously. Joining starts with a simple assessment so we can place you at the right level and build a plan around your goals.

  • Book a driver assessment on track or in the simulator
  • Meet the coaching team and discuss your season goals
  • Get a personal development and race-calendar plan
  • Flexible packages — from single events to full seasons
  • Family-friendly onboarding for young drivers and parents
  • No previous racing licence required to apply

What A Race Weekend Looks Like

On race weekends our drivers only have one job: drive. The team takes care of everything else, from kart preparation to data debriefs between sessions.

  • Karts prepared, set up and scrutineered before you arrive
  • Engineer-led briefings before every session
  • Live timing and telemetry reviewed after each run
  • Tyre, fuel and spares management handled by the team
  • Mental preparation and race-strategy coaching
  • Full debrief and development notes after the event
