The Road To Professional Racing

A step-by-step pathway designed to take you from beginner to professional driver.

1. Start Karting

Learn the basics, build confidence and fall in love with racing.

Ages 6+

2. Compete & Develop

Race regularly, improve your skills and learn race craft, data and consistency.

Ages 10+

3. Advance & Specialize

Move to national & international races. Focus on performance, fitness, and mental strength.

Ages 14+

4. Professional Pathway

Compete at higher levels, gain exposure, secure support and build your career.

Ages 16+

5. Professional Driver

Reach your goal: compete professionally and represent yourself on the world stage.

18+
